Our Story

Founded by Sam Leo, Prime Consulting Group began with a clear purpose: to fill the gap between rapidly evolving AI tools and the governance systems firms need to use them responsibly. With two decades of experience in compliance, cybersecurity, and enterprise IT, Sam has built a team that understands both the technical and regulatory challenges facing today's professionals.
