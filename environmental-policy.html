Environmental Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Prime Cybersecurity is committed to environmental responsibility and sustainable business practices. We recognize our role in protecting the environment and are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact while providing exceptional cybersecurity and IT staffing services to Canadian companies.

Environmental Objectives

Our environmental objectives include:

  • Minimizing our carbon footprint through efficient operations and remote work capabilities
  • Reducing paper consumption by promoting digital documentation and communication
  • Encouraging sustainable practices among our team members and clients
  • Supporting remote work arrangements that reduce commuting and office energy consumption
  • Continuously improving our environmental performance through regular assessment and action

Implementation

We implement our environmental policy through:

  • Digital-first operations to minimize paper and physical resource usage
  • Remote work support for our team and placed professionals
  • Energy-efficient office practices where applicable
  • Waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Regular review and updates to our environmental practices

Compliance

Prime Cybersecurity complies with all applicable environmental laws and regulations in Canada and commits to exceeding minimum requirements where possible.

Review

This environmental policy is reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect our ongoing commitment to environmental stewardship.
