Environmental Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Considering the purpose and context of the organization, and the condition of the environment in which Prime Consulting Group's operates, the company's management is committed to the continual improvement of the Environmental Management System (EMS), according to compliance obligations, by following the principles and requirements of ISO 14001:2015.

We strive to achieve the protection of the environment by preventing and eliminating environmental pollution through:

  • Continual environmental education to prevent environmental pollution, preserve natural resources, and improve the Environmental Management System processes.
  • Continual raising of environmental awareness among the personnel and third parties.
  • Making cost-effective use of energy, water, and other natural resources to minimize waste and pollution in all activities performed.
  • Decreasing non-renewable energy consumption and replacement with renewable energy sources wherever possible.
  • Considering environmental aspects and impacts that emerge from the context of the organization and the life cycle of the services provided by the business.
  • Following environmental procedures to monitor and control environmental pollution, degradation, and damage to the environment.
  • Decreasing adverse impact on water, soil, air, and biodiversity.
  • Assessing regularly the environmental impacts resulting from the organization's operations to continually improve the environmental performance.

The framework for setting quality objectives is defined in the EMS Manual.

The President is responsible for communicating the Environmental Policy to all persons working for or on behalf of the organization and making it available to the public.
